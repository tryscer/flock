2. Lock identities: Merge [[Canon/photo-rules/section1_characters]] (ages/ethnicities/attire/vibe/age-coding) + [[Canon/photo-rules/section2_portraits]] (detailed portraits). Use height/proportion chart from [[Canon/photo-rules/section4_consistency]].
