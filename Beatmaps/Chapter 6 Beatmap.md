3 tact play in which love is discovered to be a stronger drug than the usual rocknroll drug

- The Love acts as an enhancer of teen spirit: less fear, need to move, to rock.
  - The caravan breaks the segregation in the stadium not just because they don’t agree with the band’s choice, but because the Love drives them to act

- The kama-sutra character of the steam pillars fan:
  - Soft with the world and eager to cooperate
  - Restless, individualistic, eager to do and make
  - Egoless and self-assured, eager to work with others

- The science fiction engine is that this group character creates the superorganism of the flock

Leftover stuff

- Following the battle of the bands, new caravan members change the character of the caravan, while being introduced to it - drag racing.
- They creatively interpret the Green Book to find locations to stay - for the big caravan before Atlanta this will be the cliffs National Park. Later in chapter 7 we’ll see Indian reserves and mennonite farms.
- Tim writes love poems on phone booths for alice (would later become the songs)
