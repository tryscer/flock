Note: in this novel, chapters are called tracks and novel parts are called sides, mimicking an album structure. 

[[Epitaph]]
Changes:
- The chapter presents three recurring lines that frame the novel’s political, romantic, and moral conflicts.

- Alice

- Cindy notices Eleanore's lipstick marks on Tim’s neck, worsening the tension in the car.
- Sheriff Emerson stops the group after Barry’s reckless passing maneuver, but Cindy’s improvised cover and Barry’s performance get them released.
