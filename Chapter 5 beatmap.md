**\### Day 2 - June 15th \###**

1.  Eddie pushes the wonderland into the bus, telling them he’ll
    convenience mama to let them join. Mama gives them one last
    briefing. Dressing in Sunday bests in the church bus from the church
    stash. Heith is with them in the bus. Heith is giving them a good
    mood.

2.  Upheaval:

    1.  Wonderland- Barry arriving at the Jefferson square, seeing the
        band performs “He’s got the whole world in his hands” as a blind
        choir with washboard, washtub bass and a tambourine on stairs in
        Jefferson Building

    2.  When there is a critical mass, the band transitions to "Up Above
        My Head" by Sister Rosetta Tharpe. They leverage their good mood
        and start spreading it

    3.  Tim & Alice are dancing in front as if they are innocent
        bystanders, inciting others to do the same. Cindy and Elenore
        are also there dancing and singing! The boy Elenore is dancing
        with will turn out to be the son of a gun

    4.  A Traffic Policeman yells at them from his elevated booth to go
        away

    5.  They feel the “Love” and beam it around.

    6.  They walk down market street still singing, with people that
        just came out of the church, mostly young but not all, following
        them and clapping, blocking the road

    7.  They arrive at the boundary of East Market St black area

    8.  the black part of the caravan is there in the outskirts of the
        Ghetto. A piano on the curb or sidewalk, seemingly left there,
        reveals Heith playing

    9.  A big party starts, with dancing not exactly mixed but mirroring
        across the invisible line. They keep on singing and clapping.

    10. Mama

    11. The policemen on scene call for backup using the police box

    12. “Respectable citizens” led by the honorable cyclist intervene,
        creating a human block between white and blacks, tension is up.
        The cyclist sees his son in the dancing crowd and is furious,
        dragging him out. Elenore slips an invite

    13. Cindy identifies the charming cyclists and approaches him while
        Tim, Alice, Eddie and some other guy circle Villma and take her
        to Barry’s car, the rest of the band disperse one by one while
        the music continues

    14. Arriving to Barry, who is waiting steaming in the car, and he,
        Villma and Eddie are driving away, with police whistles and
        cacophony of sounds behind them

3.  In the car with Villma. Tall tales, Elenore is talking about the sun
    of a gun. Getting to know Villma as a little good girl, ambitious
    but open, with a sweet voice. She asks if they are part of the
    caravan, Barry reveals that Tim plans to return home after tonight.

4.  In the pipeworks, right as the show ends, as they are handing out
    bracelets. Seeing Elenore with the new guy. Alice & Tim in a corner,
    she convinces him to stay
